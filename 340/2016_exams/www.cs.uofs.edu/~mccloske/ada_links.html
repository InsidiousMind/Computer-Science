Recommended books on Ada 95

  • Ada as a Second Language (2nd edition), by Norman H. Cohen, McGraw-Hill, 1996.
    This may be the most comprehensive, and best, book on Ada95.
  • Rendezvous with Ada (2nd edition), by David J. Naiditch, Wiley, 1995.
  • Programming in Ada 95 (2nd edition), by John Barnes, Addison-Wesley, 1998.
  • Ada 95: Problem Solving and Program Design (2nd edition), by M. Feldman and E. Koffman, Addison-Wesley, 1997. This is a CS 1 book that employs Ada95 as the programming language.
  • Ada 95: The Craft of Object-Oriented Programming, by John English, Prentice-Hall Europe, 1997. Intended for the beginning programmer, but probably more appropriate for someone who has completed at least one programming course.
  • Object-Oriented Software in Ada 95, by Michael A. Smith, ITP, 1996.
